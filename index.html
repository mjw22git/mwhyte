#ownyourowndata

Your data is your life.
Treat it that way.

#ownyourowndata is a simple idea: your messages, files, photos, contacts, and calendar shouldn’t be the “product” that funds someone else’s business model. The goal is digital self-determination — tools that serve you, not advertisers.

What does “own” mean?

You control where your data lives, who can access it, and how it’s used. That includes encryption, clear permissions, and the ability to leave without losing everything.

Why it matters now

When your inbox and documents are readable by third parties, they become searchable, shareable, and monetizable. Privacy isn’t secrecy — it’s safety, dignity, and autonomy.

Practical wins

Less profiling. Fewer “nudges.” Less exposure if a vendor is breached. More confidence that your work and personal life stay yours.

Start small

Begin with email. It’s the master key to most accounts. Choose providers that prioritize encryption, transparency, and user control — then build from there.
